In Decatur's Food Bank we have many services that we offer to the people who need them. Some of these are:

  • Social dining: we provide lunch and dinner services at a social dining situated in downtown Decatur. This service is provided by volunteers and it is open for everyone who needs it from 12pm to 8pm every day.
  • Food distribution: we offer the option of distributing a variety of different food in boxes throughout the city of Decatur. There is a pick-up point in Downtown Decatur where our Food Bank is located. The schedule is from 9am to 9pm every day.
  • Festive meals: we offer 2 festive meals were people who are alone can share a good meal with other people and feel loved. The 2 festives that we celebrate are Thanksgiving on November 21st and Christmas on December 22nd. We also give blankets, gloves and essential things for winter.
